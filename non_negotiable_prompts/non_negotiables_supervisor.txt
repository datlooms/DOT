S.15 — THE CONCURRENCY CONTROL: 6-LOT LIVE-RISK JAR (RATIFIED; replaces the count cap):

JAR. The jar holds 6 lots of LIVE (pre-break-even) risk. Each open position occupies 1 lot of

full it is skipped. When a position reaches break-even (its stop at/through the BE lock, no
further loss possible) its 1 lot LEAVES the jar and frees a slot. No fractional sizing. HARD
BOUND: live risk never exceeds 6 lots. It counts RISK, not bodies -- break-even'd winners no
longer block new entries. Validated (1 lot, RUN TM): 2,409 trades, net $58,685, PF 5.83, WR
91.9%, worst-day -$127.5, max-DD -$165.6, 6/6 folds, 22/22 weeks, all weekdays, OOS PF 6.57 --

size (6), the 1-lot unit, the BE-frees-a-lot mechanic, and the admit-only-when-room rule.
Requires a running live-lot counter as new persistent state (+1 on open, -1 at break-even,
guarded so the decrement fires exactly once; a post-BE close does NOT decrement -- its lot

S.16 — THE EXECUTION MODEL: PER-BAR SL REPOSITIONING (RATIFIED; replaces per-tick):

check vs current SL, (2) tier update off the closed bar's high/low, (3) BE arm/lock, (4) trail.

validated per-bar book. CRITICAL DISTINCTION: the broker still holds a HARD SL that executes
intrabar on any tick -- downside protection is ALWAYS live per-tick; only the EA's REPOSITIONING
of the SL is per-bar. Per-tick repositioning is RETIRED: with LOCK_FRAC=1.0 the lock lands on
the tier-1/trigger price, so per-tick repositioning places the SL on the current price the
